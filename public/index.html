Aspen to Sheets

What is this?

Get all your grades in a simple spreadsheet and recieve email notifications whenever an assignment is posted.

Is it secure?

Not at all! Your passwords are not encrypted bacause I have no idea how to do that. If you are worried about others having access to your information definitely do not use this.

How can I use this?

  • Make a copy of this spreadsheet
  • Share the spreadsheet with service@node-js-sheets-270902.iam.gserviceaccount.com with edit access
  • Enter you Aspen username, password and the email you want notifications delivered to
  • Check the box
  • Accept any permissions Google asks for
  • Return to the sheet. It should update within 20-40 seconds!
  • Contact me for help
